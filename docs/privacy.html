How FocusWarden protects your privacy and data

üîí Our Privacy Commitment

At FocusWarden, we believe your privacy is fundamental. This extension is designed with a zero-data collection approach, meaning we collect, store, or transmit absolutely no information about your browsing activity, personal data, or online behavior.

üõ°Ô∏è Key Privacy Principles

  • No Data Collection: We collect zero user data
  • Local Storage Only: All data stays on your device
  • No External Transmission: Nothing is sent to our servers
  • No Tracking: We don't monitor your online activity
  • User Control: You have complete control over your data

üì± What Data We DON'T Collect

  • Browsing History: We don't track which websites you visit
  • Personal Information: No names, emails, or identifying data
  • Usage Patterns: No analytics or behavior tracking
  • Content Data: No access to page content or form data
  • Network Information: No monitoring of network requests beyond blocking

üíæ What Data We Store Locally

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API:

  • Blocked Website List: Domains you choose to block
  • Schedule Settings: Your time-based blocking preferences
  • Extension Preferences: UI settings and quick block options
  • Extension State: Whether blocking is enabled/disabled

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

üåê How the Extension Works

FocusWarden operates entirely on your device:

  • Local Processing: All blocking logic runs on your computer
  • No External APIs: We don't call external services
  • Chrome APIs Only: Uses only Chrome's built-in extension APIs
  • No Code Injection: No external scripts or code execution

üîê Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • Chrome Security: Extension runs in Chrome's secure sandbox
  • Local Storage: Data never transmitted over the internet
  • User Control: You can clear all data anytime
  • No Backdoors: No hidden data collection mechanisms

üìÖ Future Privacy Commitment

We have no plans to collect user data now or in the future. FocusWarden is designed as a privacy-first productivity tool. Our business model is based on providing value through website blocking functionality, not through data collection or monetization.
